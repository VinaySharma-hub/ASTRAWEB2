About Our Project

At Astra Club, we explore the frontiers of robotics and autonomous systems across land and air. Our Mars Rover features a custom rocker-bogie suspension and autonomous navigation for stable, intelligent exploration on challenging terrains. Meanwhile, our UAV and drone systems focus on aerial innovation, combining computer vision, flight stability, and GPS-based autonomy for mapping, surveillance, and exploration. Together, these projects embody Astra Club’s mission to design advanced, multi-domain robotic systems for the future.
